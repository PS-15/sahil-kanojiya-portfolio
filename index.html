About

Operations discipline.
An AI-native mindset.

I lead a team of 40 associates across four concurrent US Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management projects — Prior Authorization, Accounts Receivable, and Denial Management — at Credence Global Solutions.

Over six years, I've moved from working claims one at a time to designing how entire teams work: coaching structured development plans, resolving client escalations, and turning KPI reviews into real process change. I was promoted from Senior Analyst to Non-Designated Team Lead and, most recently, to Team Leader.

In parallel, I've been building fluency in the tools reshaping this industry — prompt engineering, AI agents, and workflow automation — with the goal of bringing that thinking back into healthcare operations, not just talking about it from the outside.
